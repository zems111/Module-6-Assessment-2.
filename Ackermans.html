Ackemans

Ackermans is a South African chain of clothing retail stores. Founded in 1916 in Wynberg, Cape Town, Ackermans has over 700 stores across Southern Africa, including in Namibia, Botswana, Lesotho, Swaziland and Zambia,[3] and is headquartered in Cape Town. In 2015 Ackermans is rated the second best clothing store by the South African Consumer Satisfaction Index.
